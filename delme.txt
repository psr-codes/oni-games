Feature Request: Show coin transfers in transaction approval popup

Hey team! I'm building a dApp on OneChain where users pay a mint fee (0.1 OCT) when minting NFTs. The transaction uses SplitCoins from the gas coin + TransferObjects to send the fee to our treasury, followed by a MoveCall.

The transaction works correctly on-chain — the 0.1 OCT transfer goes through. However, the OneWallet approval popup only shows "Network Fee" (gas cost ~0.006 OCT) and doesn't display the coin transfer amount anywhere in the summary.

This is a UX/security concern because:

Users can't see how much OCT is actually leaving their wallet before signing
A malicious dApp could hide large transfers behind a small gas fee display
Other wallets (e.g., Sui Wallet) show "Estimated Changes" including token outflows
Could the wallet popup surface SplitCoins + TransferObjects amounts (e.g., "Sending 0.1 OCT to 0x...") so users can see the full cost before approving?
